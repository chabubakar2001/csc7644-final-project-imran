Answer:
Short Answer:  
The main contribution of this paper is the development of a stochastic pavement management model (SPMM) that utilizes a nonhomogenous discrete Markov chain to optimize maintenance and rehabilitation (M&R) plans for pavement networks.

Evidence-Based Explanation:  
The paper proposes to develop a long-term, network-based pavement management model that employs a nonhomogenous discrete Markov chain. This model aims to formulate an efficient decision policy that yields an optimal set of M&R actions and their respective timings, as well as optimal M&R fund allocations over a specified analysis period [Chunk 3].

Limitations or Uncertainty:
